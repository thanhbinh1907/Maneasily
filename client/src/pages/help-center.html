Getting Started

Everything you need to know to hit the ground running.

How to create your first Project

Welcome to Maneasily! Creating a project is the first step to organizing your work.

  1. Click the + Create button on the top right of your dashboard.
  2. Give your project a name (e.g., "Website Launch").
  3. Choose a background color or image to make it stand out.
  4. Select your visibility: Private (only you) or Workspace (whole team).
Tip: You can use templates to setup common workflows instantly.

Understanding the Interface

Let's take a quick tour of your workspace:

  • Sidebar: Quick access to your favorite projects and team settings.
  • Board View: Your main workspace. Columns represent stages (To Do, Doing, Done).
  • Top Bar: Search, Notifications, and Profile settings.

You can collapse the sidebar anytime to get more focus space.

Tasks & Projects

Manage your work efficiently with powerful task features.

Adding Deadlines & Priorities

Never miss a deadline again. Open any task card to set a due date.

You can also assign priority levels:

  • ● High Priority (Urgent tasks)
  • ● Medium Priority (Regular work)
  • ● Low Priority (Nice to have)

Tasks nearing their deadline will automatically turn yellow (24h left) or red (overdue).

Using Subtasks (Checklists)

Break big tasks into small steps using Subtasks.

  1. Open a task detail view.
  2. Click "Add Subtask".
  3. Type your item and press Enter.

A progress bar will appear on the card cover showing completion percentage.

Team & Sharing

Work better together.

Inviting Members to a Board

Click the "Share" button at the top of your board. You can enter an email address or copy a secure invite link.

Roles:

  • Admin: Can delete board and remove members.
  • Member: Can edit content and move cards.
  • Observer: Can only view and comment (Pro plan).

Account & Billing

Manage your personal profile and subscription.

Resetting your Password

If you forgot your password, go to the Sign In page and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your email, and we will send you a reset link instantly.

Changing Avatar

Go to Settings > Profile. You can upload a new photo or link your Gravatar account. Maneasily supports JPG, PNG up to 5MB.

Troubleshooting

Fix common issues.

Sync Issues / Not Loading

If your board isn't loading:

  1. Check your internet connection.
  2. Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R).
  3. Clear your browser cache.

If the problem persists, please check our Status Page or contact support.
